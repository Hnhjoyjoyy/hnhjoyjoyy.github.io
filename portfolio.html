PROJECTS

Cyberbullying Awareness

The Cyberbullying Awareness Project is undertaken with the goal of combating the rising threats and adverse consequences associated with online harassment.Through strategic campaigns, educational materials, and community engagement, the project seeks to empower individuals to recognize, prevent, and respond effectively to cyberbullying, fostering a safer and more inclusive digital environment for all.

Rock,Paper & Scissors Game

The Rock, Paper, and Scissors Game Project is designed to provide a fun and interactive platform for users to engage in a classic game that transcends cultural and age boundaries.Through the development of an accessible digital version of the game, the project aims to promote recreational enjoyment, cognitive engagement, and social connectivity among users, offering a lighthearted and enjoyable experience for players of all ages.

RentRight App

The Renting Application Project is chosen to streamline and modernize the rental process, recognizing the evolving needs of individuals seeking efficient and user-friendly solutions for property transactions. The project's goal is to enhance accessibility and convenience in the rental market, making it easier for users to find, secure, and manage rental properties, thereby optimizing the overall renting experience for both landlords and tenants.
